ABOUT T.A.G CREW

Who We Are

T.A.G Crew is a passionate group of modders and developers dedicated to creating amazing experiences for the Sonic community. We specialize in developing high-quality mods for Sonic 3 A.I.R. (Angel Island Revisited), bringing new gameplay mechanics, characters, and adventures to one of the most beloved Sonic games.

Our flagship project, "Tag Team for Sonic 3 A.I.R.," introduces innovative team-based gameplay that allows players to experience Sonic 3 in completely new ways. We're committed to preserving the classic Sonic feel while adding modern enhancements and creative twists.

Our Mission

Our goal is to enhance the Sonic 3 A.I.R. experience by creating mods that:

• Introduce new gameplay mechanics and features

• Maintain the classic Sonic charm and feel

• Provide high-quality, polished experiences

• Foster community engagement and creativity

• Support the broader Sonic modding community

Current Projects

Tag Team for Sonic 3 A.I.R. - Our main project that adds cooperative and competitive multiplayer elements to Sonic 3. Experience classic levels with friends in new and exciting ways!

We're always working on new features, improvements, and additional content. Stay tuned to our Discord server for the latest updates and development progress.

Join Our Community
